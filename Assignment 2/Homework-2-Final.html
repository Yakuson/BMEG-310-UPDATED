The results are different each run because the labeling of M and B doesnt always match the diagnosis labels, when they match the accuracy is high and when theyre reversed the accuracy is low. To consistently get the correct accuracy, take the larger of the accuracy score given and 1-accuracy:

accuracy <- max(accuracy, 1-accuracy)

Then, the mean accuracy across 10 runs is the same as in Q2.1:
